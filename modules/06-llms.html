How do LLMs actually work?

Models like ChatGPT and Gemini are Large Language Models. They don't "know" facts; they predict the next piece of a puzzle.

1. Tokens vs. Words

AI breaks text into tokens (parts of words). For example, "hamburger" might be 3 tokens: "ham", "bur", and "ger".

2. Probability Engines

If you type "The cat sat on the...", the AI looks at its massive database and realizes "mat" is the most statistically likely next word.

Key Concept: AI is essentially an ultra-advanced autocomplete system trained on nearly the entire public internet.
