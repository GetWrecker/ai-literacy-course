Module 3: AI in Your Daily Life

You don't need to be a scientist to use AI. In fact, you've likely used it several times today already.

1. The "Invisible" AI

AI often works behind the scenes to make our digital experiences smoother:

  • Smart Keyboards: Predictive text and autocorrect use NLP (Natural Language Processing) to guess your next word.
  • Streaming Services: Netflix and Spotify use recommendation engines to suggest content based on your past behavior.
  • Navigation: Google Maps uses AI to predict traffic patterns and suggest the fastest routes in real-time.

2. AI as a Personal Assistant

Modern Generative AI (like ChatGPT or Gemini) acts as a "second brain" for productivity:

Daily Hacks:
  • Summarizing long emails or articles.
  • Drafting meal plans based on what's in your fridge.
  • Explaining complex topics (like taxes or physics) in simple terms.

3. Health and Wellness

Wearable devices use AI to monitor your heart rate, sleep patterns, and activity levels, alerting you to potential health issues before they become serious.
