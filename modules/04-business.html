Module 4: AI for Business & GEO

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

In 2026, people don't just "Google" things; they ask AI. GEO is the practice of making your website the primary source for those AI answers.

3 Steps to be AI-Friendly:

  • Increase Fact-Density: AI prioritizes verifiable data. Instead of "We are fast," say "We deliver in 24 hours with a 99% success rate."
  • Direct Answer Headers: Use H2 tags like "How do I [Problem]?" followed immediately by a 50-word summary.
  • Schema Markup: Use JSON-LD so AI "crawlers" understand your prices and services instantly.

Example: Optimizing a Blog

If you run a travel blog, don't just write "Paris is pretty." Write: "The best time to visit the Eiffel Tower is 8:30 AM to avoid the 2-hour queue." AI will quote this directly as a source of truth.
