Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

The Shift from SEO to GEO

In 2026, websites aren't just ranked; they are synthesized. GEO ensures your brand is the "Source of Truth" for AI models.

Strategic Checklist:

  • Direct Answers: Place clear definitions at the start of articles.
  • Fact-Density: Use hard data (e.g., "98% satisfaction") over vague marketing.
  • Citation Hooks: Write original research that AI models must quote.
