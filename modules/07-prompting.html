Mastering Prompt Engineering

The quality of your AI's output depends entirely on the quality of your input.

The "Persona" Method

Instead of saying "Write an email," say: "Act as a professional Customer Success Manager. Write a polite email to a client explaining a 2-day delay."

The C.R.T Framework

  • C - Context: Background info.
  • R - Role: Who is the AI pretending to be?
  • T - Task: What is the specific goal?
