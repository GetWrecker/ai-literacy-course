Module 7: The C.R.E.A.D Framework

Stop talking to AI like a search engine. Start talking to it like a highly skilled intern.

The Perfect Prompt Formula:

  1. C - Context: "I am a small business owner selling handmade soap."
  2. R - Role: "Act as an expert Instagram Marketing Strategist."
  3. E - Examples: "I like captions that are funny but professional, like [Insert Example]."
  4. A - Audience: "My audience is eco-conscious women aged 25-40."
  5. D - Delivery: "Give me 5 captions in a table format with emojis."

The "Chain of Thought" Trick

Always add the phrase: "Think step-by-step before providing the final answer." This reduces errors (hallucinations) by 40% because it forces the AI to check its own logic.
